Pesto Pizza

Description

Pesto pizza is a simple recipe for pizza that should only take about 20 minutes to make.

It will take 10 minutes to prepare everything and 10 minutes for cooking

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) baked pizza crust
  • 1 half cup of pesto
  • 1 ripe chopped tomato
  • 1 half cup of chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 (2 ounce) can chopped and drained black olives
  • 1 half small chopped red onion
  • 1 (4 ounce) can drained and sliced artichoke hearts
  • 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F(230 degrees C
  2. Spread pesto on pizza curst.
  3. Top with tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, red onions, artichoke hearts and feta cheese
  4. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted
