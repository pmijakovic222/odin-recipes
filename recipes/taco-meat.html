Taco Meat

Description

This recipe is only for taco meat.

It's for about 5 servings and should only take 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 half teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 half teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 half teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 half teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Hear a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt, and cumin.
  4. Pour tomato sauce over the beef, stir to coat, and simmer until thickened, slightly, about 5 minutes.
